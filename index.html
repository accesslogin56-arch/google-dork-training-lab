Important safety note

This site contains only harmless fake pages and fake sample data. It is for learning how Google search operators work without touching real systems, exposed cameras, admin panels, private documents, live services, or private files.

Google indexing something does not mean it is fair game. Only use advanced searches against your own websites, public documents, authorised labs, or systems where you have clear permission.

What this teaches

This lab safely demonstrates common search ideas such as site:, intitle:, inurl:, filetype:, exact phrase searching, exclusions, and basic discovery files like robots.txt and sitemap.xml.

It is designed for harmless learning, website self-checking, and understanding how search indexing works.
